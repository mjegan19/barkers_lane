My passion for caring for animals started from an early age. Like most, I adopted and cared for many pets growing up. Learning to care for my own canine's grooming and hygiene needs helped foster an interest in providing this service for other people's pets.

After completing a Cert III in dog grooming, I embarked upon a journey of dogscovery starting my grooming business Barker's Lane. I made it my objective to deliver cute haircuts for canines in a stress free and loving environment.

Throughout this journey, I've ensured the business is kept up to date and compliant, having attended seminars on dog behaviours and handling, all to provide your pooch with a pleasant experience in the salon!
